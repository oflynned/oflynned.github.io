Our Mission

Going Carbon Neutral Together

At PlusPlant, we are determined to overcome environmental uncertainty by offsetting carbon emissions. We work with businesses and other organisations to calculate their unique carbon footprint. We then plant, manage, and monitor forestry tailored to individual business and organisations to ensure their carbon footprint is offset.


PlusPlant forests are based in Uganda just north of beautiful Lake Victoria. Together with local communities, we plant native tree species, which are both excellent at absorbing carbon and protecting local biodiversity.


So when you see a product with the PlusPlant logo, you'll know that it is a carbon neutral product, which is doing its small part ot make sure our planet is healthy and happy!

Global warming and adverse environmental change are intensified by the release of greenhouse gases by humans. Greenhouse gases trap heat from the sun and disrupt natural balances upon which ecosystems depend. Carbon emissions, a product of combusting fossil fuels, are the largest contributor to greenhouse gases in our atmosphere. The use of energy in our homes, businesses and public work places all contribute to this problem, elevating atmospheric CO2

Forests play a unique role in the Earth’s Carbon Cycle. By means of photosynthesis, trees absorb CO2 and turn it into oxygen, which they release back into the atmosphere while the remaining carbon is stored in the trees’ hard structures such as roots, trunks and branches. These structures “lock-up” large amounts of carbon. In addition, carbon rich arboreal detritus such as leaves, twigs and bark, which fall from the trees, are continuously worked into and stored in the soil. Today, as such, forests are the biggest terrestrial ‘sinks’ of carbon, absorbing an estimated 20% of man’s emissions, this helps maintain the correct temperatures needed to sustain fragile ecosystems.

At PlusPlant, we are focused on tackling environmental uncertainty by offsetting carbon emissions. We work with business and other organizations, measuring their unique carbon footprint. Knowing and continuously monitoring the capacity of tree species and soils to absorb carbon. We then plant and manage forestry tailored to individual business and organizations, offsetting their carbon footprint. Choosing products and services, which have been carbon offset with PlusPlant, is a sustainable and ecologically friendly way of doing our individual duty in mitigating environmental degradation.

Our Beginnings

PlusPlant began in 2014 as a simple idea: to help companies adopt sustainable practices which are good for both the environment and for business. In late 2016, PlusPlant began planting on its first acre of soil!
