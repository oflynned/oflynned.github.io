Drink Tracker

Drink Tracker helps you to monitor how much alcohol you're drinking and act as a sober friend in your pocket. Say goodbye to over-drinking, hangovers, blackouts and ruined nights out.

Drink Tracker is your guide to real-time awareness, where warnings let you know in due time when enough is enough.

Neurobranch

Neurobranch is a cross-platform, cloud-based bio-research study platform designed for the small to medium sized bio/pharma institutions and academic use.

The platform allows researchers unparalleled flexibility in data acquisition and increasing retention rates of study candidates.

H. Pylori Care

H. Pylori was commissioned by the Tallaght Academic Gastroenterology Group containing guidelines on diagnosis and treatments for clinicians and patients.

The app aims to aid the treatment of the H. Pylori virus and is available for both Android and iOS devices.

LaunchBox Accelerator Programme

Tallaght AMNCH TAGG Group

Trinity Academic Gastroenterology Group (TAGG) is an active research and clinical team working collaboratively at the Adelaide & Meath Hospital incorporating the National Children’s Hospital (AMNCH) and St James’s Hospital Dublin with Trinity College Dublin.

Trinity Biomedical Institute

The Trinity College Dublin Biomedical Sciences Institute (TBSI) is an environment where innovative and interdisciplinary approaches are leading to scientific discoveries of biomedical importance, ultimately giving rise to better patient care. Our focus is on outstanding discovery.
